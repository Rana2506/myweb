Proper inspection and maintenance are vital for the longevity and reliability of your Heinrich Safety Solutions fall arrest system. We recommend:
  • Pre-Use Inspection: A quick visual check before each use for cuts, fraying, corrosion, or deformities.
  • Regular User Inspection: A more detailed inspection by a trained user at least monthly.
  • Competent Person Inspection: A thorough inspection by a certified competent person (e.g., from Heinrich Safety Solutions) at least annually, or more frequently based on usage and environmental conditions.
  • Proper Cleaning and Storage: Following manufacturer guidelines for cleaning and storing equipment in a dry, cool, and dark place away from chemicals and extreme temperatures.
Heinrich Safety Solutions offers training programs on inspection and maintenance, and we provide recertification services across India to ensure your equipment remains in top working condition.
