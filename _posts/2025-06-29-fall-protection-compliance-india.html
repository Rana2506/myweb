Fall protection compliance standards and regulations for Indian industries

Understanding Fall Protection Compliance in India

Fall protection compliance is critical for any industry working at height. In India, workplace safety regulations have become increasingly stringent, requiring businesses to implement comprehensive fall protection systems.

Key Indian Safety Standards

  • IS 3521:2018 - Safety belts and harnesses
  • IS 4130:1988 - Safety nets for construction
  • Factory Act 1948 - General workplace safety
  • Building and Construction Workers Act 1996 - Construction-specific safety
Important: Non-compliance can result in heavy penalties and legal liability for workplace accidents.

Essential Fall Protection Equipment

Personal Protective Equipment

  • Full-body harnesses (IS 3521 certified)
  • Energy-absorbing lanyards
  • Safety helmets with chin straps
  • Non-slip safety footwear

Anchor Systems

  • Horizontal lifeline systems
  • Vertical fall arresters
  • Anchor points and plates
  • Safety nets and guardrails
